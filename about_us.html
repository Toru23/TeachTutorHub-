Our Purpose

The purpose of creating this website is to provide a platform for students to access high-quality ICT (Information and Communication Technology) education. We aim to make learning about computers and technology easy and accessible for everyone, especially those in underserved communities. By offering a range of resources, including lessons, worksheets, and interactive content, we hope to empower students to develop essential digital skills and succeed in the modern world. Our goal is to bridge the digital divide and ensure that all learners have equal opportunities to thrive in an increasingly digital society.

Our History

Our website, made by Year 8 students at Azura, began because we wanted to make learning about computers more fun and easy. We noticed there weren't many resources that were right for us, so we decided to make our own. We worked hard to create lessons and activities that we would enjoy and that would help other students like us.As time went on, our website grew. We spent a lot of time researching and making content that we thought would be helpful for students. We wanted to make sure that learning about computers was interesting and not too hard.Now, our website is a place where students can learn and have fun. We're proud of what we've done so far, and we're excited to keep learning and making our website even better.
